About Project

YEPP.APP has emerged as a startup. YEPP.APP offers gifts for birthdays or special occasions. came into being to eliminate the trouble of thinking. In the profile you create on the site You list your needs and share this list with your community. From there, people can choose their own they choose the gift that suits their budget and buy it for you. In this way, you have met your own needs and the other party doesn't have to think about the gift.
